Squishling Champions

Privacy Policy — Dreamer Studios

Dreamer Studios ("we", "us", or "our") built Squishling Champions as a commercial mobile game. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Squishling Champions collects the following types of data:

  • Game progress data — your story progress, evolution shards, PvP record, unlocked characters, and upgrade loadouts are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults.
  • Game Center data — if you sign in to Game Center, Apple handles your Game Center identity. We receive your Game Center player ID for PvP matchmaking. We do not store this ID on our servers.
  • Advertising data — we use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. See Google's privacy policy below.
  • Purchase data — in-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. We do not collect or store your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To save and restore your game progress locally on your device
  • To match you with opponents in PvP Quick Battle via Game Center
  • To serve advertisements through Google AdMob
  • To process and verify in-app purchases through Apple StoreKit

Third-Party Services

Squishling Champions uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Apple Game Center — apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases) — apple.com/legal/privacy

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in Squishling Champions. AdMob may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your device and usage to deliver relevant ads.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting adssettings.google.com, or by enabling "Limit Ad Tracking" in your iOS device settings under Privacy → Apple Advertising.

Players who purchase the "Remove Ads" in-app purchase will no longer be shown advertisements. AdMob will not be initialized for those players.

Children's Privacy

Squishling Champions is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

Data Storage and Security

All game progress data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We do not operate servers that store personal player data. Cloud backup of local data (e.g. via iCloud) is handled entirely by Apple.

Your Rights

Since we do not store personal data on our own servers, most data deletion can be accomplished by deleting the app from your device. For data held by Apple (Game Center, purchases) or Google (AdMob), please refer to their respective privacy policies and account management tools.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
