Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock Spark, Nibble, and Shadow?

Spark is unlocked by completing World 1, Stage 4 in Story Mode. Nibble is unlocked by completing World 4, Stage 6. Shadow is unlocked by completing Story Mode entirely. Only Bouncy is available at the start.

What are Evolution Shards and how do I earn them?

Evolution Shards are the upgrade currency used to unlock Evolution Paths and alternate abilities. You earn them by completing Story Mode stages, winning PvP Quick Battles, and ranking up in PvP. Visit the Upgrades menu to spend them.

What are Heart Crystal Shards?

Heart Crystal Shards are collected by defeating mini-bosses and zone bosses in Story Mode. Collect all 25 to reunite the Heart Crystal and unlock the true ending of Squishling Champions.

How does PvP Energy work?

PvP Quick Battle uses 1 energy per match. Energy refills automatically — 1 charge every 12 minutes up to a maximum of 5. You can see your current energy on the main menu.

What does the Remove Ads purchase include?

The Remove Ads purchase permanently removes all advertisements from Squishling Champions for your account. It is a one-time purchase and applies across reinstalls on the same Apple ID via StoreKit restore.

How do I restore my purchases after reinstalling?

Your in-app purchases are tied to your Apple ID. If you reinstall the game, your purchases will be automatically restored when you sign in with the same Apple ID. If they don't restore automatically, please contact us.

My game progress was lost — what can I do?

Game progress is stored locally on your device. If you deleted the app, progress cannot be recovered. We recommend keeping the app installed or backing up your device via iCloud. If you believe there is a bug causing progress loss, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.

PvP Quick Battle keeps matching me with AI — is that normal?

Yes. If a real opponent isn't found within 5 seconds, the game automatically falls back to an AI challenger so you're never left waiting. As the player base grows, real matches will become more common.

What are Evolution Paths?

Each Squishling has two Evolution Paths — Path A and Path B — which unlock different ability sets and visual forms as you evolve in battle. You can unlock and switch between paths in the Upgrades menu using Evolution Shards.

The game is crashing — what should I do?

Please make sure you're running the latest version of Squishling Champions and that your device is running iOS 16 or later. If the issue persists, email us with your device model, iOS version, and a description of what you were doing when the crash occurred.
